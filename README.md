# Ecoengage
Website of ecoengage, a starup of grade 10 students
Startup Summary

Objective: This startup focuses on driving community engagement in waste management, particularly in tier 3 cities and rural areas, by providing an accessible, interactive website to foster sustainable practices.

Key Features
Interactive Map:

Users can locate nearby recycling centers, composting sites, and clean-up events.
Community members can add locations and leave reviews, making waste management resources easily accessible.

Gamification:

A points system rewards actions like recycling and participating in events.
Monthly challenges with rewards encourage ongoing engagement and a sense of accomplishment.

Community Challenges:

Monthly themes on topics such as composting or recycling.
Users log their progress, share stories, and receive recognition for contributions.

Simplified AR Features:

Basic tools for identifying proper waste disposal methods using item images.
QR codes and visual guides provide quick, accessible recycling information.
Unique Selling Points
Local Impact: Tailored to smaller cities and rural areas where waste management resources may be limited.
Community Focus: Encourages collective action and creates a sense of ownership among residents.
Ease of Use: Simplified features and accessible language make it friendly for users of all tech skill levels.
This startup combines technology and community spirit to address local waste issues, empowering users to make a difference right where they live.
