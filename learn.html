Waste Disposal Guide

Plastic Waste

Recycle at designated plastic recycling centers.

Paper Waste

Place in paper recycling bins.

Organic Waste

Compost or dispose in a green waste bin.

E-Waste

Dispose of at certified e-waste recycling locations.

Metal Waste

Recycle at scrap metal collection centers.

Glass Waste

Rinse and place in glass recycling bins.

Textile Waste

Donate usable items or recycle at textile recycling centers.

Hazardous Waste

Dispose of at hazardous waste disposal facilities.
